About ACR Platform

Mission

Enhancing early prevention and diagnosis of breast and cervical cancer through decentralised AI research while preserving patient privacy.

Technology

Combining radiomics, federated learning, and blockchain to create a secure global research network.

Impact

Processing 1,200+ cancer cases annually through our clinical validation network.

Core Technology

AI Diagnostics

Deep learning models for tumor characterisation using MRI, CT, and ultrasound imaging.

Federated Learning

Collaborative model training without sharing sensitive patient data.

OWL Ontology

Semantic framework for standardised cancer data representation.

Web3 Infrastructure

Decentralized storage with IPFS and blockchain-based authentication.

Research Framework

Radiomics Analysis

  • Automated extraction of 1000+ quantitative features
  • Intratumoral and peritumoral segmentation
  • Prediction of lymphovascular space invasion (LVSI)

Clinical Validation

  • Multi-ethnic validation cohorts
  • Integration with O-RADS scoring system
  • AUC of 0.97 in ovarian cancer detection

Privacy Framework

  • GDPR/PIPL compliant architecture
  • Zero-knowledge proof verification
  • Patient-controlled data access
