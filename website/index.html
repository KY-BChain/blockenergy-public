About BlockEnergy

BlockEnergy (BE) is a decentralised platform leveraging blockchain, AI, and DeFi to revolutionize global energy and commodity trading. BE enables Transactive Energy, petroleum product trading, and carbon credit trading with transparency and efficiency for today's Real World Assets (RWA).

Features

Decentralised Trading: Powered by Fetch.ai’s Autonomous Economic Agents (AEAs).

AI-Driven Insights: Enhanced by DeepSeek’s analytics and automation.

Multi-Currency StableCoins: CBDC-pegged StableCoins for cross-border transactions.

Regulatory Compliance: Automated compliance with EU ETS, Chinese carbon credit frameworks, and more.

Sustainability: Supporting green energy projects and EV infrastructure.

Technology

Fetch.ai: AEAs for decentralized trading and IoT integration.

DeepSeek: AI for market analysis, risk assessment, and document automation.

Blockchain: Built on Cosmos SDK for scalability and interoperability.

Web3: Decentralized hosting on IPFS and blockchain-based authentication.
